Where To

Duration: 4 weeks (November)
Where: BEPiD (Brazil Education Program for iOS Development)

I worked on this project in BEPiD.

Where To is an app for you, party people, who needs to find a good place to spend your night. Also, you can judge the place once you're there in some aspects like: queue, music, service etc.

This app is owned by Pedro XXX, and I worked as UI Designer on this project.
