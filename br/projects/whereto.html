Where To

Where To is an iOS app to people who wants to find a great place to go out. The users give scores to th places (bar, restaurant...) and with this data, it's built a list of the best places to go out.

I worked in this project while on BEPiD (Brazil Education Program for iOS Development).

This app is owned by Pedro Casagrande, and I worked as UI Designer on this project.
